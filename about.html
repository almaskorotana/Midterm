About Mellifluous

Mellifluous exists to connect designers who make beautiful, ethical and sustainable clothes made by the UK's best fashion students and graduates with the people who want to buy them.

We aim to support up and coming designers, while making consumers feel good that the clothes they're wearing were made on British soil with respect for people and the planet.
